# Helpdesk Knowledge Base

## Refunds & Returns

- Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase.
- Once approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
- Damaged items qualify for a full refund or replacement. Customers should provide photos of the damage.
- Digital products are non-refundable after download.
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a company error.

## Account Management

- Passwords must be at least 12 characters long.
- To reset your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email.
- Account deletion requests take 14 days to process. All data is permanently removed after that period.
- Users can update their email address from the Account Settings page. A verification email will be sent to the new address.
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) can be enabled from Security Settings.

## API & Technical Support

- API documentation is available at https://docs.example.com/api.
- API rate limits are 1000 requests per minute for standard plans, 5000 for enterprise.
- If you receive a 502 error, check the status page at https://status.example.com. If the service is operational, verify your API key and endpoint URL.
- API keys can be regenerated from the Developer Dashboard. Old keys are revoked immediately upon regeneration.
- Webhook endpoints must respond with a 200 status within 30 seconds or the delivery will be retried up to 3 times.

## Billing & Subscriptions

- Plans can be upgraded or downgraded at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
- Invoices are available for download from the Billing section of your account.
- We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.
- Annual plans receive a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.
- Failed payment retries occur on days 1, 3, and 7 after the initial failure. After that, the account is downgraded to the free tier.

## Shipping & Delivery

- Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the US.
- Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for an additional fee.
- International shipping takes 10-15 business days depending on the destination.
- Tracking numbers are sent via email once the order has shipped.
- Orders placed before 2 PM EST on business days are shipped the same day.

## General

- Business hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST.
- For urgent issues outside business hours, email urgent@example.com.
- Our service level agreement (SLA) guarantees a first response within 4 business hours.
